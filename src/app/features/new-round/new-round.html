What is Scramble Roulette?

Scramble Roulette is a free golf scramble game that layers a deck of cards onto classic scramble format golf. Before your foursome tees off on each hole, you draw a card that sets the rules — who hits, what the team must do, and how much chaos enters the round.

Built for 9- and 18-hole golf outings, it works as a scramble golf party game, a scorecard companion, and a way to settle debates on the fairway. One phone runs the whole round: setup your players, draw cards, enter par and score, and review your golf scramble history when you're done.
